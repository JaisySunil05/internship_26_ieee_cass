# 4. Floorplan

## Overview

Floorplanning is the first stage of the physical design process in ASIC implementation. It defines the physical layout of the chip by determining the core dimensions, placement of I/O pins, and allocation of space for standard cells and routing resources. A well-planned floorplan is essential for achieving good timing, low congestion, and efficient chip utilization.

During this stage, the synthesized RTL design was imported into **Cadence Innovus**, and an initial floorplan was created in preparation for the subsequent physical design stages.

---

## Objectives

- Import the synthesized netlist into Innovus.
- Define the chip core dimensions and aspect ratio.
- Configure I/O pin placement.
- Allocate routing space.
- Prepare the design for power planning and placement.
